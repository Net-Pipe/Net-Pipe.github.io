抛物线：二级结论

Jan 17, 2022

焦半径公式 ¶

  • $|AF|=|AA^{\prime}|=\dfrac{p}{2}+x_A$
  • $|AF|=\dfrac{p}{1-\cos\theta}$
  • $|BF|=|BB^{\prime}|=\dfrac{p}{2}+x_B$
  • $|BF|=\dfrac{p}{1+\cos\theta}$
  • $\dfrac{1}{|AF|}+\dfrac{1}{|BF|}=\dfrac{2}{p}$

焦点弦公式 ¶

  • $|AB|=x_A+x_B+p$
  • $|AB|=\dfrac{p}{1-\cos\theta}+\dfrac{p}{1+\cos\theta}=\dfrac{2p}{\sin^2\theta}$
  • $|AB|=\dfrac{2p}{\sin^2\theta}=\dfrac{2p(\sin^2\theta+\cos^2\theta)}{\sin^2\theta}=2p(1+\dfrac{1}{\tan^2\theta})=2p(1+\dfrac{1}{k^2})$

特殊角和共线 ¶

  • $\angle AHB=90\degree$
  • $\angle A^{\prime}FB^{\prime}=90\degree$
  • $\angle AKF=\angle BKF$
  • $A^{\prime},O,B$ 共线
  • $A,O,B^{\prime}$ 共线

切点 ¶

  • 以 $AF$ 为直径的圆与 $y$ 轴相切
  • 以 $AB$ 为直径的圆与准线相切

面积 ¶

  • $S_\triangle AOB=\dfrac{p^2}{2\sin\theta}$
证 
\begin{aligned}
AB:y&=\tan\theta(x-\dfrac{p}{2})\\
&\eq\tan\theta\cdot x-y+\tan\theta\cdot\dfrac{p}{2}=0\\
\\
d_{O-AB}&=\dfrac{\tan\theta\cdot\dfrac{p}{2}}{\sqrt{\tan^2\theta+1}}\\
&=\tan\theta\cdot\dfrac{p}{2}\cdot\sqrt{\cos^2\theta}\\
&=\sin\theta\cdot\dfrac{p}{2}\\
\\
S_\triangle AOB&=\dfrac{1}{2}\cdot|AB|\cdot d_{O-AB}\\
&=\dfrac{1}{2}\cdot\dfrac{2p}{\sin^2\theta}\cdot\sin\theta\cdot\dfrac{p}{2}\\
&=\dfrac{p^2}{2\sin\theta}\\
\end{aligned}
